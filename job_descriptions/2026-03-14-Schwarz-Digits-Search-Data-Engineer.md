## Full Job Description Text
### Your tasks
- Create Global Impact: Design and maintain production-ready RESTful services (FastAPI) that connect millions of users worldwide with the information they need.
- Collaborate at the Intersection of AI: Work closely with ML Scientists to integrate advanced models into our search pipelines, ensuring our solutions are as reliable as they are innovative.
- Design Smart Data Flows: Develop and optimize the ETL/ELT processes that power our Elastic Cloud clusters, with a meaningful focus on data freshness and search relevance.
- Cultivate Seamless Deployments: Support our CI/CD pipelines and "Infrastructure as Code" to ensure our deployment processes are safe, efficient, and high-performing.
- Nurture System Health: Implement robust logging and observability tools to ensure the long-term stability and health of our global systems.
- Empower the Team: Contribute to our engineering best practices and support your colleagues through collaborative code reviews and shared learning.

### Your profile
- Python & Backend Craftsmanship: You take pride in writing clean, reliable code. With a solid foundation in Python and SQL, you value maintainability through type hints, linting, and thoughtful unit testing to ensure long-term stability.
- Designing for Connectivity: You enjoy the creative process of designing and maintaining RESTful services. You feel comfortable navigating command line/shell environments and use Git to collaborate effectively with your peers.
- Building Cloud-Native Solutions: You bring hands-on experience with GCP (or similar) and an interest in modern architectures, including containerization (Docker, Kubernetes) and event-driven systems like Pub/Sub.
- The Search Mindset: You are curious about (or already experienced in) Elasticsearch/OpenSearch. You understand that how we structure data directly shapes the user’s experience and search performance.
- Commitment to Clarity: You believe that great documentation is the backbone of a successful team, making it easier for everyone to understand, contribute to, and scale our shared projects.
- Collaborative Spirit: You thrive in an environment where communication is as vital as coding. You enjoy listening to business needs and translating them into elegant technical solutions that move the whole team forward.
- Inclusivity & Language: Our primary language is English. While German is a nice addition, it is not a requirement—we value your skills and perspective above all else.
