- Knowledge of distributed OLAP datastores (Druid, ClickHouse)
- Hands-on ELK stack experience for log aggregation

**Preferred Qualifications:**
- Big data ecosystem experience (Hadoop, Hive, Spark)
- Software engineering best practices understanding
- Complex problem-solving orientation
- Passion for enabling analytics and machine learning through quality data
